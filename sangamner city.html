Sangamner Map

Sangamner is a city and a municipal council located in the Ahmednagar District of Maharashtra state in India.

It derives its name from the site of the sangam (confluence) of three rivers in the area: the Pravara, Mhalungi, and Adhala.

Sangamner is one of the most developed and largest cities in the nearby area and is famous for its markets. It has become an in dustrial hub in recent years, with industry in the city including clothing, sugar, and tobacco factories. Its market is famous for clothes, jewellery and even electronics. The city is also a hub for milk-processing units and has one of the district's biggest marketplaces.

Sangamner has become an educational centre with numerous colleges and schools. Most of the colleges are affiliated with Pune University and have numerous libraries in the city itself. The city boasts good school education and has amazing facilities for higher education in law,medicine, engineering, pharmaceuticals, dentistry, etc.

The city enjoys a central location (at the centre of Mumbai, Pune, nashik and Aurangabad) & is only 2 hours from Nashik, 3 hrs from pune & 4 hrs from Mumbai.

Amrutvahini Sanstha


Amrutvahini Sheti & Shikshan Vikas Sanstha was founded in 1978 by the visionary leader Doyen of the cooperative
movement and great philanthropist, the late Hon. Sahakar Maharshi Bhausaheb Santuji Thorat. In the late 1970s,
Sangamner was a backward region plagued by social evils like poverty, illiteracy, drought, and unemployment.
Hon. Bhausaheb Thorat was determined to change this situation and believed education was the key to progress.
He envisioned a Sanstha that would provide affordable and accessible education to the poor, regardless of their
caste or creed. The Sanstha was established with the vision of providing quality education and technical training
to the rural masses of Sangamner taluka in Ahmednagar district, Maharashtra.

His legacy is well carried forward by his Son, Former Revenue Minister and AICC invitee Member, President of
Amrutvahini Sheti & Shikashn Vikas Sanstha Hon. Shri. Balasaheb Thorat Saheb and equally supported by Former
MLC and Trustee Hon. Dr. Sudhir Tambe Saheb. The path laid by all these great visionary leaders is well kept
and marched forward by his granddaughter, Trustee Hon. Mrs. Shrayu Ranjit Deshmukh Madam.
